About Olivia

Olivia Klipfel is a rising Senior at Pitzer College, majoring in Integrated Media Technology and Innovation, a self designed major that brings together education within both computer science and media studies.

When not working towards her education, Olivia remains a part of the varsity swim team for the Pomona Pitzer Sagehens, as well as keeping up with her hobbies of hiking, surfing, and playing games with her friends.

Her coursework delves into various aspects of technology, focusing on how creativity and computability can mesh together to create something truly special.
